Gastón P. Fernández

I am a Ph.D. student at the Economics Department of KU Leuven, Belgium. My advisors are Laurens Cherchye and Frederic Vermeulen.

I visited the Department of Econometrics & Operations Research at Tilburg University.

My research is in applied microeconomics with a special emphasis on the fields of Family Economics and Child Development. I am also interested in studying the impact of Technological Change on labor markets and firms’ behavior.

I will be on the 2024/2025 academic job market.
